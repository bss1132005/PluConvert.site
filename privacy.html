Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains what information we collect (and what we don't) and how we use it.

The Core Principle: We Don't See Your Files

PlusConvert is a serverless, client-side application. The core functionality of file conversion happens entirely within your web browser on your computer. We do not upload, store, or transmit your files to our servers at any point. This means we have zero access to your data, ensuring the highest level of privacy and security by design.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you choose to sign up for a premium plan, we collect your email address and payment information. This data is handled securely by our payment processor (e.g., PayPal) and is used solely for managing your account and subscription.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous and aggregated usage data, such as which conversion formats are most popular or the number of files processed in a batch. This data contains no personal information and cannot be tied to a specific user or file. We use it solely to identify popular features and improve our service.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, see, store, or have access to your files or the content within them.
  • We do not track your activity across other websites.
  • We do not sell or share any user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time. Since we do not store your files, there is no file-related data for you to manage or delete from our systems.
